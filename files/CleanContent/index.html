About CleanContent

CleanContent is a tool that helps Instagram users automatically moderate comments using AI. It connects to your Instagram Business account and enables you to detect and remove inappropriate or harmful comments, improving your brand's online presence and engagement.

Tech & Integration

Our app uses Meta's Instagram Graph API and only processes data for the authorized user. We do not share or retain platform data. Permissions requested are solely for displaying comment data and allowing users to take moderation actions from our app interface.
